# Craps-Game
Craps is a dice game where you roll two dice and try to win based on the result.

- How to play
1. Roll 7 or 11 - You win  
2. Roll 2, 3 or 12 - You lose 
3. Roll anything else - That number becomes your goal number


- Keep Rolling:
1. Roll your goal number again - You win 
2. Roll a 7 before your goal number - You lose
