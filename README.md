# Craps-Game
Craps is a dice game where you roll two dice and try to win based on the result.

How to play
Roll 7 or 11 - You win  
Roll 2, 3 or 12 - You lose 
Roll anything else - That number becomes your goal number


Keep Rolling:
Roll your goal number again - You win 
Roll a 7 before your goal number - You lose
